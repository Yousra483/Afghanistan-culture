Kabul

The capital of Afghanistan

Kabul:is the largest city and the capital of Afghanistan. It is located in the eastern mountains of the country and holds great historical,Cultural,and political importance . kabul is one of oldest cities in central Asia,with a history spanning more than 3,500 years. It has been mentioned in ancient texts as early as the 2nd millennium BCE Due to its strategic Location along trade routes between South and Central Asia,Kabul has been a cultural and political center for centuries.

General Information:

  • Country:Afghanistan

  • Population:Over 4 million people

  • Location:A valley surrounded by mountains,with cold winters and hot summers

  • Languages:Dari and Pashto

Pashtun beauty

Pashtun people are known for their natural beauty,bright eyes,and balanced features. with their colorful traditional clothing,they add a unique charm to Afghan cuiture.

Tajik beauty

Tajik people have delicate features,with almond-shaped eyes and weel-defined eyebrow. Their fair skin and warm smiles add a unique charm to beauty of Central Asia.

Hazara beauty

Hazara people have distinct Eastern features,with narrow eyes and delicate bone structure that give them a unique beauty. Their warm smiles and sincere gazes reflect their rich heritage and authenticity.
